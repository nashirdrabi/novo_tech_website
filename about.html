Executive Summary

Building Expertise and Innovation in Infrastructure Development

NovoTech Consultants is a budding team of Engineers, Designers, Architects, Interior Designers, Surveyors, and support staff with substantial experience and necessary skill sets in infrastructure development, including Design Engineering, Architectural Consultancy, Geo-Tagging, Environmental Engineering, Hydropower Engineering, Project Management Consultancy, Topographical Surveys using ETS, Geotechnical Investigations, and preparation of DPRs, PFRs, GIS & DTP.

The NovoTech Consultants team is uniquely qualified to provide comprehensive services that modern infrastructure projects demand — built upon our strong combination of knowledge, practical experience, teamwork, and capabilities. Our project team has significant expertise in engineering, architectural, and interior designing consultancy across the Kashmir Valley and Jammu region.

The firm is presently engaged in various projects involving comprehensive engineering, planning, design, survey, and project management of multi-faceted developmental schemes across the state. We also collaborate with specialized sub-consultants in related domains — such as field and social surveys, geotechnical testing, and environmental monitoring — enhancing our overall knowledge base and technical strength.
