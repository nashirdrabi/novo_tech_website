About Us

A Creative Engineering Consultancy for Your Dream Project

NOVOTECH CONSULTANTS is a budding team of Engineers, Designers, Architects, Interior designers, Surveyors and support staff with substantial experience and necessary skill set in the infrastructure development e.g., Design Engineering, Architect Consultants, Geo tagging, Environment Engineering, Hydropower Engineering, Project Management Consultants, Topographical Survey using ETS, Geo Technical Investigation, Preparation of DPR’s and PFR’s, GIS & DTP. The NOVOTECH CONSULTANTS team is uniquely qualified to provide the comprehensive services that Infrastructure Development Projects demand, based on our combination of knowledge, practical experience, team resources and capabilities. The NOVOTECH CONSULTANTS project team has significant experience in the field of engineering and architectural and interior designing consultancy in the Kashmir Valley and Jammu region. The team is presently working on various projects involving Comprehensive Engineering, Planning, Design, Survey and Project Management of multi- faceted developmental schemes under various jurisdictions of the state.
